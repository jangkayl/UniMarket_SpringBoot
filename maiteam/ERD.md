# Unimarket ERD (Entity Relationship Diagram)

This diagram represents the database schema for the Unimarket application, including all tables, fields, and foreign key relationships. Use this as a reference for future questions or changes.

---

![Unimarket ERD](erd.png)

## Key Entities & Relationships

- **STUDENT**: student_id (PK), student_number (UK), university_email (UK), ...
- **MESSAGE**: message_id (PK), sender_id (FK), receiver_id (FK), item_id (FK), ...
- **ITEM**: item_id (PK), seller_id (FK), ...
- **TRANSACTION**: transaction_id (PK), buyer_id (FK), seller_id (FK), item_id (FK), ...
- **REVIEW**: review_id (PK), reviewer_id (FK), reviewee_id (FK), transaction_id (FK), ...
- **DISPUTE**: dispute_id (PK), complainant_id (FK), respondent_id (FK), transaction_id (FK), ...
- **LOAN_REQUEST**: loan_id (PK), borrower_id (FK), lender_id (FK), ...
- **PAYMENT**: payment_id (PK), payer_id (FK), payee_id (FK), transaction_id (FK), loan_id (FK), ...
- **NOTIFICATION**: notification_id (PK), user_id (FK), ...
- **BADGE**: badge_id (PK), ...
- **STUDENT_BADGE**: student_id (FK), badge_id (FK), ...

---

Foreign keys are indicated by (FK), primary keys by (PK), and unique keys by (UK).
